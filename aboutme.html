About Me

Hi, my name is Tokoni and I write poetry.
Here's my story...


Tl;dr: Voices in my head tell me poems, and I write them down.


I’ve been hearing voices, before all I heard was silence…
Or maybe not silence, maybe I just wasn’t paying attention to the voices in my head, the voices reciting poetry.
I’ll never forget the first time I remember paying attention to the voices in my head.
Close your eyes,

Picture this:
It’s the middle of the day,
There’s a boy seated by the window of a sun-lit Fine Art lab.
The day is bright, it’s bright and fair; a normal day like every other day before that day.
The boy is surrounded by people, not so much though, but these people include at least one classmate of his and the Fine Art teacher.
The boy his ~8 years old, he’s in Year 8.
The boy reads a lot, and maybe he’s reading now as he’s seated by the window;
When suddenly he opens his mouth and begins to recite this poem:

I was going to the market,
One early Winter morning
When I saw a little bunny
Sticking out it’s little tail.
Then I saw it do it a wiggle
And it ran around a spot;
But I have to keep on going
Because mother told me to.
The boy doesn’t know it but he’s just written his first poem.
The thing is he didn’t write it down and he didn’t actually recite it, he sang it.

Literally out of nowhere, that poem came to me.
You see, mostly as a result of voices in my head that speak to me without my volition, I have been writing poetry since I was 8.
